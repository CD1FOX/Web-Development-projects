Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mini donuts

Is there a warranty on your donuts?

While we don't offer a warranty (since they're food items), we guarantee your satisfaction! If you're not happy with your order, let us know and we'll make it right.

How long do the donuts stay fresh?

Our mini donuts are best enjoyed within 24 hours. For maximum freshness, we recommend eating them within 4-6 hours of purchase.

Do you have a return policy?

Since our donuts are perishable, we can't accept returns. However, if there's any issue with your order, please contact us immediately and we'll be happy to help!

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery within a 5km radius. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location.

What are your store hours?

We're open daily from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, because good donuts should be available all day!

Do you make custom orders?

Absolutely! We'd love to help with your special event. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for custom orders.
